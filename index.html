About Me

I am a Biomedical engineer studying the cause and effects of seizures in epileptic patients. Responsible for source localization of seizure onset and 3D reconstruction of images for surgical planning.

Further I'm also involved in a multi-site research study on SUDEP. Sudden unexpected death in epilepsy (SUDEP) is a fatal complication of epilepsy. It is defined as the sudden and unexpected, non-traumatic and non-drowning death of a person with epilepsy, without a toxicological or anatomical cause of death detected during the post-mortem examination.

The work I do will help pave the way on finding a cure for epilepsy and identify biomarkers for at risk patients.
